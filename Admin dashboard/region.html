Key Insights

  • Cluster 1 drives 52% of total CO₂ savings with only 28% of the customer base.
  • Cluster 2 can double impact by converting single-use buyers into repeat refill users.
  • Cluster 3 needs hyperlocal engagement and retail tie-ins to boost refill adoption.
  • Cluster 4 requires grassroots outreach, subsidies, and infrastructure support.

Next Steps
